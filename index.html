ABOUT ME

Entrepreneur, Traveler, Father, Surfer, and Language Learner: In June of 2019 after a long career in mortgage and non-profit housing development, my wife and 8-year-old son and I decided to take a year off and travel the world. I had previously started a small “Healthy Vending” company as “side gig” and together with renting our home on Airbnb, teaching English to Chinese children online, and a small amount of savings as a backup, we hoped to come close to breaking even financially each month and maybe even become able to fund our travels indefinitely.
While we were living in our first stop in Cuenca, Ecuador we came across many full-time travelers that “code” and work remotely. At their suggestion, I began to investigate a few languages like JavaScript, HTML, and CSS found that the learing process was very similar to I had been doing to prepare for our travels. I also loved the challenge and the process of coding, but soon found that I needed a bit more structure in order to really wrap my arms around the process and this is where UCF Coding Bootcamp came in. I love work and I am looking forward to seeing where it takes me while exploring the world.
